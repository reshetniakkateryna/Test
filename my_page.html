Short biography

I was born on February 14, 1988 in the beautiful Polish town of Legnica. My parents were military men and we often moved. When I was 5 years old we moved to Ukraine to the city of Kharkov. I consider this city to be my homeland and love it very much. In Kharkov, I grew up, left school and university, met many friends. Now I'm happily married and have 2 naughty sons. Family takes an important part of my life.

Education

I entered the school in 1994 and graduated in 2005. In 2010, I graduated from Kharkiv National Automobile and Highway University with a degree in civil engineering of roads and airfields.
